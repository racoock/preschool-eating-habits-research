# Preschool Eating Habits Study

## Abstract
This study examines the correlation between water consumption and food choices among preschool children.

## Introduction
Proper hydration is crucial for children's health and may influence their dietary preferences.

## Methods
We collected data from 10 preschool children, recording daily water intake levels (Low, Medium, High) and fruit servings consumed.

## Results
Statistical analysis revealed a positive correlation between water intake and fruit consumption.

## Discussion
Children with higher water intake tended to consume more fruits, suggesting a possible link.

## Conclusion
Encouraging water consumption may promote healthier eating habits in preschool children.
